The Story

Side Curtains started the way most things in the British car world start ‚Äî with enthusiasm, a vague plan, and the quiet certainty that it would take longer than expected.

The idea is simple: create a welcoming place on the internet for people who love post-war British roadsters. Not a commercial venture, not a slick media platform ‚Äî just a spot where enthusiasts and restorers can share stories, swap advice, and appreciate these wonderful, maddening, beautiful machines together.

The name? If you've ever fumbled with a set of side curtains in a sudden downpour, you know. They're flimsy, fiddly, and never quite keep the rain out. But they're part of the experience ‚Äî part of the deal you make when you choose a car that prioritises joy over practicality.

That's what this site is about. The joy.
